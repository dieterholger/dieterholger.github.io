How much jet fuel has each US state consumed per year in the 21st Century?

More than 2 million people fly every day in 21st Century America, according to the US Federal Aviation Administration. Consumption of jet fuel is an indicator of where people are flying to and from the most.

The US Energy Information Administration (EIA) reports the amount of jet fuel consumed in each of the USA's 50 states by year. The following interactives display the most recent data from the EIA API from the year 2000 on.
